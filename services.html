NAPS & NATS

End-to-end apprenticeship program management.

ABS provides comprehensive support across the National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) and National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) — from awareness and portal registration to data management, returns, invoicing and stipend release.

  • Awareness, registration and complete portal onboarding
  • Data management, reports, returns and periodic updates
  • Invoicing, stipend liaison and BTP training-cost reimbursement
  • Training-center and job-role approval support
  • Skill examinations and certificate assistance

Key benefits under NAPS & NATS

  • No PF, ESIC, gratuity or other incentives payable for apprentice trainees
  • Employer incentive of 25% of stipend, up to ₹1,500 / ₹4,500 per apprentice each month
  • Enroll 2.5%–15% of total employee strength (permanent + contractual)
  • CSR budget can fund apprentice stipends and service charges
  • Reduced attrition and multi-location, pan-India enrollment
